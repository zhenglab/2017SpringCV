
Description:

The tar file contains all codes and results of Assignment 2 for image enhancement and restoration.


Operating Environment:

1.System environment: Windows 7 or Ubuntu 16.04 system.

2.IDE: MATLAB R2012a/R2015b

Special Reminder:

1.The whole dataset is too big to upload on github. As a result, there are just a part of processed images stored in cv_dataset. The original images obtained by do convolutions on 15 clear images and the first blur kernel image. The processed images are obtained by processing the original images with ten methods.

2.You can obtain the whole dataset from the original images by the provided codes. The following three codes need to change the size of the estimated blur kernel: L0 Sparse Representation, L0-Regularized and Dark Channel Prior.
